How It Works

Non-invasive skin tightening at Marjan DaVinci uses monopolar radiofrequency (RF) energy to gently heat the deeper layers of the skin. This precisely controlled thermal energy causes existing collagen fibres to contract immediately, while simultaneously kick-starting the body's own collagen-remodelling process over the weeks that follow.

Because the energy reaches well below the surface — targeting the collagen-rich dermis and underlying connective tissue — results go beyond what topical products or superficial treatments can achieve. Skin becomes noticeably tighter, smoother, and more resilient as fresh collagen and elastin gradually replace older, weakened fibres.

Cellulite: A Closer Look

Cellulite affects the vast majority of women and many men, regardless of body type or fitness level. It occurs when fibrous tissue bands beneath the skin pull downward on the surface while fat cells push upward, creating the characteristic dimpled, uneven texture.

Radiofrequency skin tightening is one of the most effective non-invasive approaches for smoothing cellulite. The controlled heat energy simultaneously shrinks fat cells, loosens rigid fibrous bands, and thickens the overlying skin through new collagen production — addressing the root causes rather than merely masking the appearance. Most patients notice a visible improvement in skin texture and smoothness after a series of sessions.

Skin Tightening FAQs

What does a skin tightening session feel like?

Most patients describe the treatment as a deep warming sensation — similar to a hot-stone massage. The device's built-in cooling system keeps the skin surface comfortable throughout the session. There is no anaesthesia required and no recovery time afterward.

How many sessions will I need?

For facial tightening, many patients achieve noticeable results in as few as 1–3 sessions. Body areas and cellulite typically respond best to a series of 4–6 treatments spaced 1–2 weeks apart. Your practitioner will recommend a schedule based on your individual concerns and skin condition.

Can skin tightening really help with cellulite?

Yes. Radiofrequency energy addresses cellulite at its structural level — heating and loosening the fibrous bands that create surface dimpling, while thickening the skin above. Clinical studies have shown measurable improvement in cellulite grading after a series of RF sessions. Results continue to improve for several weeks after the final treatment as collagen remodelling progresses.

Is there any downtime?

There is essentially no downtime. You may notice mild warmth or a slight pink flush in the treated area immediately after the session, but this typically fades within an hour. You can return to your normal routine — including exercise — right away.
