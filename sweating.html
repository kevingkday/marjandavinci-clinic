How It Works

Botulinum Toxin Type A (commonly known as Botox or Dysport) is a naturally occurring, highly purified protein. It works by temporarily relaxing the specific facial muscles that cause wrinkles and lines to form when you smile, frown, or raise your eyebrows.

By reducing the repetitive movement of these muscles, the overlying skin is allowed to smooth out. With consistent treatments, deep lines can gradually soften and fade, while new lines are prevented from forming, leaving your skin looking remarkably refreshed and serene.

Common Areas Treated

Frown Lines (Glabella)

The '11' lines between the eyebrows that appear when concentrating or frowning.

Forehead Lines

Horizontal lines spanning across the upper forehead when raising eyebrows.

Crow's Feet

Fine lines at the outer corners of the eyes that appear particularly when smiling.

Lip Flip & Smoker's Lines

Subtle lip enhancement and smoothing of vertical lip lines around the mouth.

Sweating (Hyperhidrosis) FAQs

Does the injection hurt?

Most patients report only minor, brief discomfort. We use the finest medical-grade micro-needles available, and the treatment only takes a few minutes. If you're sensitive, we can easily apply a topical numbing cream beforehand.

Will my face look frozen or unnatural?

Absolutely not. Our philosophy at Marjan DaVinci is 'artistry meets precision.' We specialize in administering exact, customized doses that relax targeted wrinkle-causing muscles while allowing you to maintain your beautiful, natural facial expressions.

What is the aftercare?

Aftercare is extremely minimal. We advise keeping your head upright and avoiding touching the injected area, strenuous exercise, and alcohol for the first 24 hours. You can return to your normal daily activities immediately.
