Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect during my first consultation?

Your first visit involves a comprehensive skin analysis and a deep dive into your aesthetic goals. We discuss medical history, lifestyle, and create a roadmap tailored specifically to your needs.

How long do dermal filler results last?

Typically, results last anywhere from 6 to 18 months depending on the product used and the area treated. Metabolism and lifestyle also play a factor in longevity.

Is laser hair removal permanent?

Laser hair removal provides long-term hair reduction. Most patients experience a significant and permanent decrease in hair growth after a full course of treatments.
